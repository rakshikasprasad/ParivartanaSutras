Seshan Guruji’s Afterword

These 64 qualities are inter-related and non-negotiable. All the qualities are equally important. They define a total harmony in which enlightenment happens effortlessly.

The practice of these qualities is the greatest prayer, the truest worship. Each of the terms used by the Divine, highlights a certain attitude, the value for which must be discovered personally through SAMYAMA so that each attitude becomes a natural aspect of the seeker's mind.

Constant remembrance, contemplation and cultivation of these qualities will result in excellence and perfection of character.

One has to allow these qualities to blossom inside oneself by attention and not try to enforce them. The practice of these qualities will catapult an aspirant towards his goals.

The Divine will bestow on the practitioner radiant health, immense wealth, inherent wisdom, great love, infinite knowledge, incomparable fame and ineffable beauty.

In short, YOU WILL HAVE THE POWER TO GET WHATEVER YOU WANT.
