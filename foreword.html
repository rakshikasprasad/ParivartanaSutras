Seshan Guruji’s Foreword

One sure method of transforming oneself from a human being to the Divine Being (Potential Being) is the cultivation of the qualities described in this book.

By cultivating these qualities, one can make things happen and thus become master of one's own destiny. All the universal forces will become co-operative and one will effortlessly attract luck and good fortune.

These qualities are both inwardly regenerative and outwardly practical. They will take care of our worldly prosperity and spiritual progress.

After a little sincere practice, these qualities will evolve naturally in the hearts of those who attune themselves to the Divine Mother.

Selfless dedication to the Divine Mother will take care of everything in our lives.These qualities represent the summation of all spiritual practices.

It was possible to finalise them after nearly 60 years of study and research. The names of the qualities are taken from the Bhagavad Gita, the Yoga sutras of Patanjali, Lalitopakhyanam and Valmiki Ramayana.

In the beginning, meditate on each quality for a minute. Let the seed of each quality sink into you. Also if any quality is violated at any time during daily life, become aware of the violation. Rectify and regulate yourself then and there with a resolve to see that such violations do not recur.

Place these qualities first and foremost in your lives and enjoy peace, patience, kindness, happiness, goodness and gentleness.

Prof. P.A. Seshan (Amritapadananda)
